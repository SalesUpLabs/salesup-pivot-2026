## TL;DR

We are pivoting SalesUp from a **seat-based SDR / cold-outreach agency** to an **AI-native Growth-as-a-Service partner**. Higher-ACV bundles, better margins, global reach (IN / US / EU / AU), repeatable AI workflows productized as SKUs. **Min margin floor: ₹30-40K/mo per deal.** Goal: 2-3x ACV, 27-31% margin, fewer-but-bigger clients.

This memo locks: (1) positioning, (2) ICPs + geos, (3) capability stack, (4) AI-native product SKUs, (5) pricing (IN + International), (6) what we need from tech to ship this.

## 3. Capability Stack — The 4 Pillars

We sell a **system**, not channels. All offerings group under 4 buyer-legible pillars:

| Pillar | What buyer gets | Underlying capability |
|---|---|---|
| **🧲 Attract** | Demand that flows in | **Paid ads** (Meta + Google + LinkedIn) + landing pages / microsites |
| **📣 Engage** | Demand that flows out | **Email automation** (Instantly) + **LinkedIn automation** (HeyReach) + **WhatsApp automation** + SDR (human) + AI Calling (CallKaro) |
| **🎯 Convert** | Demand → revenue | **Sales process competence** — we design and operate the client's sales process end-to-end: ICP/TAM (Blitz/Hatch), lead scoring & routing, CRM workflows, ABM, sales enablement, attribution, reporting |
| **⚙️ Automate** | The system that compounds | **Custom AI workflows** built per client (Claude + MCP + n8n + Sybill) — workflows.io-tier moat |

The 4th pillar (**Automate**) is the new differentiator. Everyone else sells channel execution. We sell channel execution **+ bespoke AI workflows** that stitch the client's stack together.
