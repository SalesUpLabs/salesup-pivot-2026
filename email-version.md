## Engage → Close → Renew flow (with RevOps)

This is what we operate end-to-end for the client. Most agencies stop at "lead booked." We run the full pipeline.

| Stage | What happens | Who does it | RevOps deliverables |
|---|---|---|---|
| **1. Engage** | Outbound across email + LI + WA + AI Call + SDR | SalesUp pod | List hygiene, sequences live, daily reply triage |
| **2. Qualify** | Inbound + outbound responses → AI qualifier → BANT score → meeting booked | AI + SDR | Lead scoring model, routing rules, calendar integration |
| **3. Meeting** | Discovery call recorded (Sybill) → AI brief → handoff to client | Client AE (or our pod for managed) | MBR scorecard, meeting → opp conversion tracking |
| **4. Opportunity** | Stage progression in CRM, follow-ups, demo scheduling | Client AE + our SDR support | CRM hygiene, stage-velocity reporting, stuck-deal alerts |
| **5. Close** | Negotiation, contract, signed | Client AE | Win/loss analysis, pipeline-to-revenue attribution |

| **7. Expand / Renew** | Upsell signals, account growth, renewal motion | Client CS | NRR dashboard, expansion pipeline reporting |

## Pricing flex — the rate card vs floor

The sticker prices above are the **rate card** (anchors). Sales can flex within rules:

| Discount lever | Allowed | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Annual prepay | -10% auto | Cashflow advantage |
| Strategic logo (e.g., LIXIL, big SaaS) | -20% max | Yash approval; case study rights required |
| Multi-tier bundle (retainer + vertical project) | -10% on project | If both signed together |
| Multi-quarter commit (6+ mo) | -5-10% | Beyond min commit |
| **Hard floor** | **₹30K / $400 margin/mo** | **Pricing tool blocks below this — no override** |

**Closing pattern:** anchor at rate card → buyer pushes back → offer Pilot or annual prepay → if still resistant, downgrade tier (don't discount sticker on same tier — train buyers it's flat).
