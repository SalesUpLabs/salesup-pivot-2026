> Compiled 2026-04-30. Research input for SalesUp's own inbound/attention engine post-pivot. Sources: 4 parallel research agents covering creators, companies, geo/platform variance, edge tactics. Full source links in each section.

## Scope note (read first)

The pivot decision is locked: **SalesUp does NOT offer Content, SEO, creators, or brand-identity as services.** This research is for **SalesUp's own marketing engine** — how we drive inbound for the new pivot offering — not a productized AI Pod service line.

## TL;DR — the big shift

**AI behind the scenes + human voice on the surface wins.** Pure-AI output (auto-posts, AI BDRs, AI-only ads) is being punished by platforms (LinkedIn 360Brew demoting AI text) and audiences (Coke/McDonald's NL pulled AI ads). AI for production/research/personalization wins.

For SalesUp specifically:
- LinkedIn AI ghostwriting: research + outline only, human voice on surface
- Reddit + LinkedIn corpus building → become the cited expert in ChatGPT/Perplexity (AEO is the new SEO)
- Click-to-WhatsApp + AI voice funnel = biggest underrated channel for Indian SMB clients (CPL -30 to -60%)
- AI-Pod-as-service is wrong fit for SalesUp B2B SaaS clients (talking-head AI fails trust bar). Right fit = ads-services upsell only

## 1. The AI-attention landscape — what's documented

### Solo creators / personal brands

| Play | Real numbers |
|---|---|
| LinkedIn/X ghostwriting (Cole+Bush GhostwriterGPT, Welsh matrix, Postwise) | Welsh: $8M solo, 1.5M followers. Twitter ghostwriter pricing $500–10K/mo per client |
| Newsletters on Beehiiv | $30M ARR platform; The Neuron 0→500K subs, exited Jan 2025 |
| Faceless YT | Top tier $1–10K/mo solo (claims of $50K+ are 100-person studios). Stack: Hedra+ElevenLabs+Nano Banana |
| Build-in-public w/ Claude Code | Pieter Levels $138K/mo Nov 2025. Counter-trend: top indie hackers going dark — copycats clone in a weekend |
| AI-as-research-engine | Reddit RAG → LLM synthesis → ICP language → hooks/copy. Now standard prep |

**Punished:** fully AI thought leadership, AI avatars pretending to be people, AI voicemails at scale, generic listicle slop.

### Companies / SaaS

**Content engines:**
- **Programmatic SEO** gutted by Google's Mar 2026 update unless paired with first-party data (Causal.app got nuked)
- **AEO is the new SEO.** ChatGPT 2B queries/day. AI-referred traffic +527% YoY, **converts 4.4× better**
- Tools: Profound, Athena, Otterly. Listicles = 32% of all LLM citations
- Comparison/alternative pages cornerstone bottom-funnel (LLMs cite balanced pages)
- llms.txt — ship it (1hr) but 8 of 9 sites saw zero traffic lift

**Paid ads — "creative as targeting":**
- 80% perf work = creative ops, 20% media buying
- 30+ creatives/month maintains CPA at scaled spend
- AI UGC: $2–20/video vs $150–2K human
- Verified case: fintech on Advantage+ Dynamic Creative — **+40% ROAS, -82% CPA**
- Tools: Arcads ($16M seed, 6K clients), HeyGen ($100M ARR, 175+ languages)

**Outbound — the AI BDR cancellation wave:**
- **50–70% cancellation** on AI-SDR contracts (11x.ai, Artisan)
- 11x — TechCrunch alleged customer-list inflation; LinkedIn rate-limited Artisan's Ava
- Clay+Smartlead works (Rippling 2× cold-email perf, Qrew +40% meetings)
- **Lesson for SalesUp:** Position pivot as hybrid (human SDR + AI augmentation), explicitly NOT full-autonomy AI BDR. Market burned itself on the autonomy pitch. ✅ Already aligned with locked positioning.

**Free tools as lead magnets** beat ebooks 70%+ (calculators, audits, generators).

## 2. Channel-by-channel cuts

### LinkedIn (most relevant — where Yash builds personal brand)
- **360Brew LLM ranking demotes AI text** with em-dashes, "It's not X, it's Y" structures, emoji-bullet patterns
- Posts that pulled thousands of impressions now get hundreds
- Welsh, Acosta, Grant publicly distanced from pure-AI workflows
- **Working pattern:** AI for research/outline → human voice on surface. Visuals/carousels dominate; plain-text dying
- Tools usable: Taplio, AuthoredUp, Supergrow for ideation/scheduling, NOT generation

### Instagram (separate from FB)
- Reels = 2× reach of any other format. Static is dead
- AI image feeds working: Midjourney → Nano Banana for character consistency (95%+)
- Beauty/skincare punishes synthetic models (Guess -12pp sentiment)
- Best stack: Midjourney + Nano Banana + Submagic + Hedra for talking-head

### Facebook (older demo, MORE AI tolerance)
- 35–65+ audience tolerates AI UGC better than IG/TikTok
- Long-form video ads (60–90s) with AI talking-head + AI B-roll work — Reels can't run this format
- Verticals where AI UGC outperforms human UGC: insurance, finance, health, real estate, local services
- Best stack: Arcads/HeyGen + Advantage+ Shopping Campaigns

### TikTok
- Symphony Creative Studio (free AI ad gen in TikTok Ads Manager) — heavy SMB adoption
- Symphony Avatars (Q4 2025) for paid ads
- AI live shopping in SEA: cost/hour 80–90% lower than humans, conversion drops vs real hosts but 24/7 coverage

### X / Twitter
- Grok 3/4 integration (Premium+ $40/mo)
- AI-thread industrial complex via Typefully/Tweet Hunter
- B2B working: Grok-assisted research + human-written replies in niche communities

### YouTube
- Jul 2025 policy update demonetizes AI slop. Late 2025: 11 high-volume AI channels terminated
- Hybrid wins: AI B-roll over human-written/narrated main content
- YT now #1 LLM citation source — long-form interviews are highest-leverage AEO play

### WhatsApp (huge for India/SEA/LATAM — most relevant to SalesUp client base)
- Standard funnel: Meta ad → click-to-WA → AI agent qualification (AiSensy/Wati) → AI voice (Smallest/Bland) for hot leads → human closer
- **CPL improvement: 30–60%** vs form+phone
- AiSensy ~14K customers, Wati 12K+, Gallabox ~6K SMBs (India)
- Meta WhatsApp Business Calling API (GA late 2025) — Smallest.ai, Bland.ai plugging in

### Reddit
- AI detection norms aggressive — mod tools auto-remove obvious GPT
- Long-form genuine narrative wins; AI used only for editing
- Reddit licensed data to Google ($60M/yr) + OpenAI — quality posts become AEO play

## 6. Direct application to SalesUp pivot

### For SalesUp's own attention engine (inbound for the pivot offering)

Top 5 highest-leverage moves:

1. **AEO + Reddit/LinkedIn corpus building** — cheapest new discovery channel. Get cited in ChatGPT/Perplexity for queries like "best SDR-as-a-service India," "AI SDR vs human SDR." Worth 90-day test alongside existing SEO plan. Set up Otterly ($29/mo) or Profound for tracking.

2. **Yash personal brand on LinkedIn** — research+outline via Claude, human voice on surface. Welsh content matrix. Carousels > plain-text. Already in marketing plan; reinforce with anti-AI-detection guardrails.

3. **Vibe-coded micro-tools on salesup.club:** "Outbound ROI Calculator," "SDR Cost Comparison," "AI Calling Cost Estimator." Each = AEO bait + lead capture. Build 1/week on Lovable.

4. **Publish "salesup-benchmarks" MCP server** — anonymized SDR/meeting/conversion benchmarks. Free distribution to every Cursor/Claude user. Zero competitors doing this in B2B lead-gen.

5. **NotebookLM weekly "B2B Outbound Briefing"** — feed it Sybill notes + industry news → 10-min audio. Publish to Spotify/Apple. Signature content, zero production.

### Anti-positioning (potential differentiator)

In a market where every cold email is GPT'd: **"Hand-written by a human SDR"** as A/B test on Instantly campaigns. Aerie's "Made by Humans" pattern lifted engagement +28%. Worth a quarterly test.

### What NOT to do (per pivot lock)

- ❌ Productize "AI Pod" / AI content service for B2B SaaS clients — talking-head AI fails their trust bar
- ❌ Build content/SEO/creator services as a retainer line
- ❌ Position SalesUp as full-autonomy AI BDR — market burned on this; align with hybrid positioning ✅

## 7. Where this research has gaps / what to validate next

- **CPL improvement claims (30–60%)** for click-to-WhatsApp + AI voice come from vendor case studies (Inc42 self-reported). Need our own pilot data — propose a 1-client test with an existing Indian SMB.
- **AEO citation lift** for Indian B2B agency category is uncharted — no benchmark. Set up Otterly + measure baseline before optimizing.
- **AI BDR backlash** is documented in US market. India SMB tolerance may be higher (less detector-savvy buyers). Don't extrapolate without local data.
