## The frame: Growth Partner

> ***"You're growing. You have a team — or you don't. Either way, we plug growth workflows into your stack. Pick a channel we run end-to-end, or pick specific workflows that augment your existing team. Packages from ₹25K to ₹5L."***

**Key insight:** Most funded companies already have SOMETHING running. We don't need to displace their team — we layer in. This expands TAM massively.

## 3 channels (everything maps here)

Growth comes through one (or more) of:

1. **Outbound** (cold email + LinkedIn)
2. **Ads** (Meta + Google + LinkedIn)
3. **Calling** (SDRs dialing the client's leads / ICP / inbound — under the hood)

> Note: We don't pitch as "cold calling agency" externally. Calling is one of three channels under the Growth Partner umbrella.

## 3 modes (the network of packages)

| Mode | When | Sticker |
|---|---|---|
| 🔌 **Plug-In** — single workflow augments client's existing team | They have ad ops / SDRs / outbound but want specific layers | ₹25-75K/mo per workflow |
| 🚀 **Channel** — we run an entire channel end-to-end | They don't have / don't want the team | ₹2-3L/mo per channel |
| 🌐 **Stack** — multi-channel + RevOps | Series B+, want one growth partner | ₹3-5L/mo |
