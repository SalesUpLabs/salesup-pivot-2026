## Problems & Pain Points
**Core problem:** B2B companies need pipeline from multiple channels (ads + outbound + AI calling + content), but the in-house build takes 6+ months and ₹50L+, while traditional agencies only solve one channel and don't own outcomes.

**Why alternatives fall short:**
- **SDR agencies:** one channel (calling/email), seat-based pricing, no ads/content/RevOps
- **Digital marketing agencies:** ads-only, no outbound/calling/follow-up
- **In-house build:** 6-month timeline, ₹50L+ cost, needs a VP Sales + tools + training
- **Tools (Apollo, Clay, Instantly solo):** software without the humans/ops to run it
- **Fractional VP Sales:** strategy without execution capacity

**What it costs them:**
- Lost pipeline quarters → missed revenue targets → missed fundraise milestones
- 3–6 months wasted on hires that don't work out
- Tool sprawl (10+ subscriptions, nobody operating them)
- Agency churn cost (re-briefing every 6 months)

**Emotional tension:** Founders feel they're "always one SDR / one campaign / one hire away" from the pipeline they need. Constant anxiety about channel mix, agency quality, and whether any of it will actually book meetings.

- Belkins / Martal / RevenueZen — outbound-heavy SDR agencies with SaaS varnish; channel-narrow

- Fractional VP Sales consultancies — strategy without execution
- Full-service digital marketing agencies — ads-only, no outreach muscle

**Indirect:**
- Referral partnerships, events, personal networks — unpredictable, doesn't scale
- Zero outbound, pure inbound content plays — 12+ month time-to-pipeline

**How they fall short:** All alternatives are either (a) single-channel, (b) non-executing, or (c) priced on seats not outcomes. Nobody bundles ads + outbound + AI calling + RevOps under one retainer with accountability for pipeline.

## Differentiation
**Key differentiators:**
- **All channels, one retainer** — ads, email, LinkedIn, AI calling, WhatsApp, RevOps under one contract
- **Custom AI workflows per client** — we don't just run channels, we build bespoke workflows (lead scoring, enrichment cascades, inbound orchestration, dealer onboarding, re-engagement, creator ops) using Claude, MCP, n8n, Blitz, Hatch. This is the workflows.io-tier differentiator.
- **AI Calling (CallKaro)** — nobody else bundles human-like AI voice calling with managed SDR + digital
- **India cost structure** — US-grade execution at Indian rates; 3–5x cost advantage on SDR muscle
- **AI-native ops stack** — Sybill, Claude, MCP, Blitz, Hatch, HeyReach, Instantly, Gamma in production
- **Outcome-priced** — retainers tied to meetings/pipeline/revenue, not seats
- **Proprietary portal** — reporting layer that looks like a CFO dashboard, not a call log
- **Vertical-aware packages** — SaaS, D2C, enterprise brand, services, local — each with its own proven playbook

**How we do it differently:** Every other agency builds one channel well. We build the *system* — ICP → ads/content pull → outbound push → AI calling follow-up → meeting → CRM → report — and operate it end-to-end for one monthly fee.

**Why that's better:** The buyer gets pipeline, not seats. One invoice. One scorecard. No tool sprawl, no agency-hopping.

**Why customers choose us:**
- Faster than hiring (live in 2 weeks vs 3–6 months)
- Cheaper than in-house (₹2–10L/mo vs ₹50L+ build)
- Broader than a single-channel agency (5 channels vs 1)
- More accountable than tools (humans + AI operating them, not DIY)
